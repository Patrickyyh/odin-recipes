Colselaw

Description

A creamy coleslaw dressing that can be made with ingredients you already have! You can pour it immediately over a 14-ounce package of coleslaw mix or refrigerate until needed.

What Is Coleslaw Dressing Made Of? his creamy, mayo-based dressing uses ingredients you likely have on hand: sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, pepper, and of course, mayonnaise. The resulting dressing is just the right balance of sweet, tangy, creamy, and peppery! ayonnaise: For a creamy coleslaw, as opposed to vinegar-based, mayonnaise is essential. Use your favorite store brand or make your own! ugar: A sweetener is necessary to balance out the tang of the vinegar and the acidity of the lemon. This recipe calls for standard white sugar, but honey or maple syrup may also be used. emon juice: Not all coleslaw dressing calls for lemon juice, but it helps to amp up the flavor and add some brightness. inegar: Vinegar is the primary source of coleslaw's signature tang. Use either apple cider vinegar or white vinegar. alt and pepper: Bring it all home with a little salt and pepper! No need to get fancy with seasonings, the simplicity of this recipe is what makes it so good.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Store in an airtight container.
